Key Facts

  • 56 teams from 9 states attended, for a total of around 900 high school students.
  • 171 undergraduate and graduate volunteers from 20 academic departments volunteered.
  • Team of 26 organizers from 12 academic departments planned the tournament.
  • Only college-run invitational tournament in country to waive registration fees.
  • First year to offer travel stipend, giving out over $2800 to 6 different schools so they could afford travel costs and academic materials.
