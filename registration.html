Registration

  • Registration will close on Thursday, October 15th, 2020 at 11:59 PM ET.
  • Each attending school must be a registered member of their state Science Olympiad organization and have paid all applicable membership fees prior to tournament day.
  • Limit of 56 teams on a first-register first-serve basis.
  • The first 10 schools to successfully register two teams will receive two slots each in the tournament. Subsequent schools to successfully register will receive one slot each in the tournament.
  • Teams will be placed on the waitlist in chronological order after the registration limit has been reached.
  • Head coaches will receive email confirmation upon submitting the registration form. If confirmation has not been received within 48 hours, please email us at scioly@princeton.edu.
  • A $100 penalty will be imposed for every confirmed team that withdraws after Friday, October 18, 2020. There is no fee otherwise for registering and/or participating in the tournament.
    • This system is in place to ensure that teams admitted off the waitlist have adequate notice in advance. It is also to encourage general commitment to attending our tournament.
